Cherry Blossom Beauty

When we hear the word sakura, we surely immediately imagine a pink
Japanese flower that is beautiful to look at. However, the fact is that
cherry blossoms are not only beautiful to look at, but also have the
ability to be one of the basic ingredients for your facial skin care

Benefits of Cherry Blossoms for Face

Since centuries ago, Japanese women have used cherry blossoms to treat skin because it is believed to have many benefits. Apparently, this has also been supported by several studies conducted by scientists. Here are the benefits of cherry blossoms that are believed to be good for the skin:

1. Brighten the skin

2. Maintain skin elasticity and softness

3. Anti aging

4. Relieves irritation

5. Shrink pores
